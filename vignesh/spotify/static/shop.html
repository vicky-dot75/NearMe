Welcome to Babu Sah! Explore the finest collections in Kanchipuram A.S. Babu Sah in Kanchipuram is a renowned silk saree store, established in 1974, offering exquisite bridal and traditional collections. With its own handloom factory and generations of expertise, the shop is celebrated for authentic Kanchipuram silk craftsmanship
